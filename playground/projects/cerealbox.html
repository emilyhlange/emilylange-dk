Designing a Cereal Box [2021]

The final design is presented below, including few animations to give the design life.


The aim of this course was to design a cereal box. First we were given a piece of media that would work as our source of inspiration. I was given "Sex and the City". From there, we created a moodboard, inspired by the media. There was full freedom for interpretation. The moodboard laid the groundwork for the final design. My design for the cereal box was heavily inspired by the New York City feeling in fall, where cocktails and cigarettes were the norm.
