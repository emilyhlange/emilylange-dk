Re-design of a multivitamin bottle [2018]

This project was done in collaboration with Kristina Fabrin Jakobsen. The project took place over the course of 3 weeks with class critiques of the design iterations each week. We initially decided to work with a multi-vi- tamin botttle when we went to the store and struggled finding a vitamin bottle that suited our needs. We then realised there was a larger issue regarding how the information is displayed on the bottles.
