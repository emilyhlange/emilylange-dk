Tune

Year — 2021 Type — Graphic Design

04 Tune

A 16-page editorial spread treating song lyrics as visual material. Each spread is a different translation of a song — not illustration, but atmosphere.

The Brief

From a graphic design course at Krabbesholm Højskole: choose texts, and give them form. Lyrics made sense — they already carry rhythm, tempo, and emotional register. The question became: what does a song look like when you strip the sound away?

The Work

Each of the 16 pages corresponds to a different track. The visual language pulls from a shared digital vocabulary — computer interfaces, terminal aesthetics, pixel grids, system typography, the textures of four decades of computing. Not every spread directly illustrates the lyrics; some are about the mood underneath the words, the signal in the static.
