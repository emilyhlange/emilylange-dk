Tune [2021]

'Tune' was created as part of a Graphic Design Course at Krabbesholm Højskole. The assignment was to create a 16-page-spread inspired by a text. The texts I chose, were lyrics from various songs. Each page is a different visual representation of a song, or the lyrics from the song. The designs are rooted in a somewhat ‘digital’ atmosphere, being inspired by computer elements that we use/have used the last 40 years. Not all pages are a direct reference to the song, but rather inspired an ambience for the 'tune' in question.
