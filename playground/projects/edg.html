• East Decatur Greenway [2018]

  • poster campaign

  • for
  • eastdecaturgreenway.org
  • their mission: from gas station to green space.

  • tools
  • adobe illustrator
  • adobe indesign
  • This project was part of a visual design course at Georgia Tech University, where I took an exchange semester. The aim of this project, was to come with two possible poster suggestions. The posters had to present the newly designed community park in East Decatur, Georgia. I targeted the poster for children, which is why the poster has a playful feel. The poster also had to include information about a special event that would be held in the park on Earth Day. I designed the poster in two parts: 1) a rip-off piece, to inform the community about the upcoming event and 2) a playful poster that could be placed more permanently.
