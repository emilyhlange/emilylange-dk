EcoDistricts [2018]

Brand Identity for ecodistricts.org. Their mission is "to mobilise, empower and accelerate sustainable and equitable urban development leadership throughout North America and beyond".

This project was part of a Visual Design course at Georgia’s Institute of Technology. Our assignment was to re-brand a selection og organisations. I chose to work with EcoDistricts that aim to create a new urban development model that ensures sustainable neighbourhoods. I conducted research on the company, including reading through their Protocol, that inspired for the brand identity booklet. Since it was a class in Visual design, the focus was on extracting the essentials of the organisation’s brand, and condensing that into a new visual identity.
