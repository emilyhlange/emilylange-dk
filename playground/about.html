I am a digital designer, with interest an in User Experience (UX) and User Intercace (UI) design, webdesign, graphic design, interaction design [IxD], and much more.

I have experience in (creative) project management and concept development of digital design products. I have a keen interest in digital design - working with the combination of the arts and the technical - the creative and the practical. My portfolio is constantly under development, and expanding in several different directions. I am building upon my passion for webdesign through freelance jobs, and finding new passions by exploring new technologies.

I am a graduate fron the IT University of Copenhagen in Digital Design and Interactive Technologies. After finishing my studies in 2021, I immediately committed to studying Graphic Design at Krabbesholm Højskole, which has been followed by a job at Design Matters (Design Matters brings together more than 1000 UI and UX designers and design leaders from all over the world, and gathers them at a conference consisting of design talks, workshops, discussions and socializing in Copenhagen.). At Design Matters I work as a UX Project Lead, launching a new platform, as well as other activities realting to upcoming conferences.
